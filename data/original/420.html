Automatic transmission

Shifting from P to R

왘 With the vehicle at a standstill, depress

the brake pedal and keep it pressed.

왘 Move gear selector lever up past the

resistance point to select reverse

gear R.

왘 Release the brake pedal.
왘 Carefully depress the accelerator pedal

to drive off when it is safe to do so.

Shifting from P to D

왘 With the vehicle at a standstill, depress

the brake pedal and keep it pressed.

왘 Move gear selector lever down past the

resistance point to select drive

position D.

왘 Release the brake pedal.
왘 Carefully depress the accelerator pedal

to drive off when it is safe to do so.

If you want to select park position P with
